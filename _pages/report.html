Looking to report a player? Please take the following information into consideration.

Video proof usually required

For almost any offense, we will only ban people if you have video proof.

The exception to this rule can be chat-related offenses (make sure to provide enough context).

Respect the decision

We ask that you remain kind and civil in these reports.

If the report gets closed, the evidence was not sufficient.

Thanks for your hard work

The staff appreciates the time you take to record/post video proof of rule breakers.

Thanks for your commitment to making {{ site.title }} a better place!
