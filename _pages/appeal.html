Please take the following information into consideration.

What can be appealed?

If you have been caught cheating, your appeal will be denied.

If there was a mistake, or the ban was issued a long time ago, we may consider giving you a second chance.

How are appeals resolved?

The {{ site.title }} staff will review the evidence, and remove the ban if it is deemed reasonable.

Privilege, not a right

Playing on our servers is a privilege.

If you abused or have shown promise to abuse {{ site.title }}, your appeal will be denied.


Appeal

The appeal process is handled on our Discord server. Please create an account if you do not already have one.
